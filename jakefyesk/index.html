Cura
My Team's Goal

An all-in-one solution for companies, automating tasks on their end using insights from their customers, generated across the customer's digital life.

What We Made

We created a chrome extension using Bootstrap, jQuery, and JavaScript as well as a backend in Flask which manages the application's data.
This backend, hosted on the Google Cloud, serves as an API for the front end and develops useful data from a user's activities, pushing them to the user through our chrome extension.

What It Could Offer

As it stands now, Cura is one part back-end automation of processes that would have traditionally required lengthy communication between users and companies, and another part browser extension which intelligently offers access to said automation.

Harmonia
My Team's Goal

We hoped to help the companies establish better workplaces, without contributing increasingly invasive software to the workplace.

What We Made

For potential employees, we developed a Slack app, which encouraged potential employees to utilize our webapp, consisting of a cognitive measuring mini-game and poll for their emotion.
For potential managers and corporate bodies we developed a separate webapp which transformed collected data into meaningful information, easy to understand.

What It Could Offer

We offer unrivaled access to indicators of their team's stress and emotions at work, and an in-depth flagging system to mark risky manager/employee relationships.
Data and graphics make the UI simple and convenient to use, while greatly empowering manager's arsenal of knowledge to boost team's performance.
We also offer employees an avenue for their feelings and stress to be heard, without feeling like they are expressing a complaint or dissent.
